# Mitsuki — Japanese Flashcards & Dictionary App

Mitsuki is a Japanese learning app for Mac and iPhone that helps you look up words, save them as flashcards, and remember them through spaced repetition.

It is built for learners who want a quiet, focused way to study Japanese from real examples, anime subtitles, dictionary lookups, and their own saved vocabulary.

Website: https://mitsukiapp.me

## What Mitsuki does

Mitsuki helps you move from lookup to memory.

You can search for a Japanese word, understand its meaning, save it as a flashcard, and review it later. The goal is simple: make Japanese vocabulary stay with you.

## Features

### Japanese dictionary lookup

Search Japanese words in kanji, kana, or romaji. Mitsuki gives you the reading, meaning, word type, and example sentences in a clean interface.

### Flashcards with spaced repetition

Save words as flashcards and review them over time. Mitsuki is designed to help you remember vocabulary instead of only looking it up once and forgetting it.

### Review mode

Review saved words with a focused card interface. Mitsuki keeps the word, reading, meaning, examples, and related study tools close together while you review.

### Learn from anime subtitles

The Mitsuki extension is designed to help learners save words from Japanese subtitles while watching. A subtitle line can become a flashcard with the word, scene, audio, and context included.

### JLPT practice

Mitsuki includes JLPT practice from N5 to N1, with timed sessions and clear results.

### AI study tools

Mitsuki Pro adds optional AI-powered study tools, including:

- AI study sessions based on your own saved words
- Custom AI decks from topics or documents
- Higher daily limits for AI features
- Naturalness checks and Japanese explanations

### Business Japanese

Mitsuki also includes tools for practical Japanese, including keigo, email phrasing, meetings, interviews, and workplace expressions.
